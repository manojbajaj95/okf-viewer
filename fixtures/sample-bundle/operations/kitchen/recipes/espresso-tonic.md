# Build

1. Fill a 12 oz collins glass with ice.
2. Add 120 ml tonic water.
3. Pull a double espresso (18 g in, ~36 g out) directly onto the tonic.
4. Express lemon peel over the glass; discard peel.

Beans come from [Alpine Roasters](/operations/vendors/alpine-roasters.md). High-volume days show up in [orders](/data/warehouse/tables/orders.md) under the `espresso-tonic` SKU.
